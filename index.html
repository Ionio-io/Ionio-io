The Ionio-nization Process

We have a standardized process to get MVPs built. This is what we love doing. This is what we specialize in. We call this the Ionio-nization Process.

  • Step 1 - Setting the Product Scope

    Our product designers work with you to create a vision for the product you are trying to build. We understand the problem, analyze the complexity, and establish a scope for a project & resoures required.

  • Step 2 - Designing the Mockup

    Working together with our UI/UX team we create a mockup of the app with all of its screens. This is how the finished MVP product will look & function.

  • Step 3 - Assign a Dedicated Team

    We assign a specialized dedicated team to work on your project. Typically this consists of a frontend React engineer, a backend NodeJS/Python developer & a project manager.

  • Step 4 - The Development

    Development time is usually 8 to 11 weeks. From week 2/3, we deliver weekly updates, as Looms videos and catchup calls. We also try to keep the scope concentrated & defer drifting away from the original idea.

  • Step 5 - Ongoing Support & Maintenance

    After the MVP development is complete we move to a retainer. Re-establish the scope, the new requirements, & create bi-monthly milestones. The same team continues to add additional features, support and maintain the app.

Going from a rough idea to fully deployed revenue-generating Saas usually takes us about 8 to 12 weeks.

Python
